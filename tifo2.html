The Stockholm Stadion Tifo

In summary, for yesterday's derby tifo at Stockholm Stadion's 110th-anniversary celebration, the original setup idea was switched to a 45 x 15 meter overhead flag. The background evolved into an arch, incorporating a 62 x 7.5 meter banner with the text "Stockholms Olympiastadion." Projectors and paint were used for the Stadion and halftime overhead sketches, while other components were sprayed outdoors. The tifo, celebrating the brick fortress, was laid out in the stands before the match.
