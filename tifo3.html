The Headline Tifo

About the the tifo from Sofialäktaren Instagram"When the pandemic disrupted tifo activities in 2020, Sofia Tifo found itself with an ongoing project for a home premiere that ended up being played without an audience. After a 1.5-year pause, the "headline tifo" was executed in the last home match of the season, featuring league-winning titles from 1966, 2002, and 2019. The arrangement included three detailed headlines flanked by arches, 12 cut-out cardboard stars (one for each league title), and two fabric stripes displaying all the golden years. This visual narrative encapsulates Djurgården's 130-year history and envisions a shimmering future. Despite the on-field game, the focus was on the successful choreography and the appreciation from the Djurgårds family.
